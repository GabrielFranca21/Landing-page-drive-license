About me:

Hello, my name is Alexandre.
If you are newly arrived in Canada or are considering obtaining a Canadian Driver's License, this post is for you. Having a Canadian Driver's License (G) doesn't just mean 'Having the Right to Drive a Car in Canada,' this document is very important for you to initiate some valuable processes here in Canada.

Benefits:

  1. Credit Score: When you obtain a Driver's License, automatically, your information is included in the Canadian financial system and in the well-known Credit Bureaus, similar to Brazil's Serasa Experian. (Considering that you don't have permanent residency or a SIN). This will help you significantly to get credit, finance a car, obtain credit lines for buying a house, and many other banking facilities. This means that you start building your 'Credit Score' here, where responsible payers earn points that increase their numbers in the Credit Score month by month.
  2. Traveling across the Country, Event Entry: The Driver's License is accepted as if it were a Canadian ID, so you can use it confidently instead of carrying your passport everywhere...
  3. Renting a property: With this document, you have more credibility to support your rental application for a good property. Many agents don't even accept passports.
